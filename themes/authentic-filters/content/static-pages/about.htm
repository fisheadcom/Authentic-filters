About Us


Authentic Filters is a locally owned & operated company that specialises in providing professional commercial kitchen cleaning services and filter exchange programs.

WHO WE ARE & WHAT WE DO

Our Services


We specialise in servicing commercial kitchens by providing kitchen filter exchanges, canopy cleaning, rangehood cleaning and full exhaust system cleaning - incorporating the flue or duct and extraction fan.

Our Customers


Authentic Filters assist a wide range of industries including restaurants, pubs, clubs, hospitals, shopping centres, sporting arenas and service centres.

Our Staff


Authentic Filters technicians and contractors are industry trained, OH&S accredited and have specific accreditation for work safety at heights. When visiting your business, our staff will do their best to avoid disruption to your operation and they will clean up prior to leaving your premises.
