Our Services

We specialise in servicing commercial kitchens by providing kitchen filter exchanges, canopy cleaning, rangehood cleaning and full exhaust system cleaning - incorporating the flue or duct and extraction fan.

Our Customers

Authentic Filters assist a wide range of industries including restaurants, pubs, clubs, hospitals, shopping centres, sporting arenas and service centres.

Our Staff
